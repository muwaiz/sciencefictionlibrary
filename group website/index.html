About Science Fiction

  • Science fiction is a sci-fi/fantasy genre that usually deals with innovative and imaginative themes such as advanced science and technology, discovery of space, time travel, alternate worlds, and alien life. It has been dubbed the "literature of ideas" which also discusses the possible implications of advances in research, culture, and technology.

  • Related to fantasy, horror, and superhero literature, science fiction, whose origins go back to ancient times, includes several sub-genres. Between writers, commentators, academics, and readers, its precise meaning has long been contested.

  • In much of the world, science fiction, film, television, and other media have become popular and prominent.
